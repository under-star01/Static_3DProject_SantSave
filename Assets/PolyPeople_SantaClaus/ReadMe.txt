■ PolyPeople Series - Santa Claus

Thank you for downloading this asset!

---

■ Package Contents

- Meshes: 2 FBX files
  - 1 with armature (rigged)
  - 1 without armature (non-rigged)

*The non-rigged FBX file can be used with auto-rigging tools.


- Materials: 2 total
  - Standard: 1  
  - Shader Graph: 1 

*The "CustomColors" material (Shader Graph) allows you to adjust skin, hair (beard), and clothing colors directly from the Inspector.


- Prefabs: 2 total
  - 2 prefab variants per character (based on material type)
  - All configured with Humanoid rig

- Textures:
  - 1 base color texture (256x256)

- Scenes:
  - 1 demo scene showcasing all prefabs

---

■ Geometry

Character model is 702 triangles. 

---

■ Requirements

- Unity 6.2 or later (tested with Unity 6000.2.8f1)  
- Universal Render Pipeline (URP) compatible
